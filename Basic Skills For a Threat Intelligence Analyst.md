A threat intelligence analyst is a cybersecurity professional who studies potential and active cyber threats to help an organization defend itself.

They collect, analyze, and interpret information about things like:

    Hackers and cybercriminal groups
    Malware and ransomware
    Phishing campaigns
    Vulnerabilities in software or systems
    Attack methods used against organizations
    Indicators of compromise, such as suspicious IP addresses, domains, files, or behaviors
